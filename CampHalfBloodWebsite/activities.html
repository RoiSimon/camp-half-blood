Camp Half-Blood Activities

At Camp Half-Blood, there’s always something exciting to do! Here are some of the most popular activities:

Archery 🏹

Perfect your aim and precision under the guidance of the best archery instructors in the camp. Whether you're a beginner or expert, there's always a challenge waiting!

Chariot Racing 🏎️

Race through the camp's obstacle course, testing your speed, reflexes, and teamwork. A great way to build your skills and have fun!

Climbing 🧗

Scale the giant walls and cliffs around Camp Half-Blood. You'll need strength, skill, and determination to conquer the climbing challenges!

Combat Training ⚔️

Learn the art of battle from experienced instructors. Whether it's sword fighting, hand-to-hand combat, or tactical strategy, you’ll sharpen your warrior skills here.

Dragon Riding 🐉

Feel the wind in your hair as you ride a dragon through the skies. A truly magical experience that only demigods can enjoy!

Swimming 🏊

Test your swimming skills in the camp’s lake. But be careful, it’s not just the water you’ll have to watch out for!

Monster Fighting 👹

Train to fight various mythical creatures in a controlled environment. Only the bravest campers dare to face the monsters!

The Annual Quest ⚔️

Every year, a select few campers are chosen to go on a quest to protect the camp and the gods. It’s dangerous, but also the highest honor for a demigod!

Hiking 🥾

Explore the dense forest surrounding Camp Half-Blood. Be on the lookout for mystical creatures, ancient ruins, and hidden treasures!

Healing 🍃

Learn how to heal wounds and ailments using herbal medicine and magic. Every demigod should know how to take care of their own!
