Cabins of Camp Half-Blood

Each cabin at Camp Half-Blood houses demigods born of a specific god or goddess...

Cabin 1 – Zeus ⚡

Home to the children of Zeus, god of the sky. Campers possess powers over weather, lightning, and flight. Strong, natural-born leaders.

Cabin 2 – Hera 👑

This honorary cabin is empty, as Hera is a goddess of marriage and does not have demigod children.

Cabin 3 – Poseidon 🌊

Campers here can breathe underwater, talk to sea creatures, and control water. Includes Percy Jackson himself.

Cabin 4 – Demeter 🌾

Children of Demeter control plants and agriculture. Skilled in growing, harvesting, and manipulating flora.

Cabin 6 – Athena 🦉

Home to wise strategists, inventors, and scholars. Known for intelligence, logic, and fierce battle planning.

Cabin 7 – Apollo 🎯

Archers, healers, and poets. Children of the sun god are artistic and deadly with a bow.

Cabin 8 – Artemis 🌙

An honorary cabin for the Hunters of Artemis. They are immortal maiden warriors and do not stay often.

Cabin 9 – Hephaestus 🔧

Masters of forging, mechanics, and technology. Known for building amazing weapons and traps.

Cabin 10 – Aphrodite 💘

Charming, beautiful, and persuasive. Experts in social skills, appearance, and love spells.

Cabin 11 – Hermes ✉️

Originally the catch-all cabin for unclaimed demigods. Home to the clever, quick, and mischievous.

Cabin 12 – Dionysus 🍇

Children of the god of wine and madness. Known for wild creativity and unpredictable behavior.

There are also cabins for minor gods and unclaimed campers.
