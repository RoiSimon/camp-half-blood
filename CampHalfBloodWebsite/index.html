Welcome to Camp Half-Blood

Camp Half-Blood is a training ground for demigods, located on Long Island Sound, hidden from mortal eyes by the Mist. Here, young demigods learn to harness their powers, train for quests, and find a place where they belong.

Our Mission

At Camp Half-Blood, our mission is to train, protect, and empower young demigods to embrace their heritage, master their abilities, and build lifelong bonds.

Camp History

Founded over 3,000 years ago by the god Chiron and the Olympians, Camp Half-Blood has served as a safe haven for demigods across North America. Hidden in the woods of Long Island, it's protected by powerful magic that keeps monsters—and mortals—at bay.
